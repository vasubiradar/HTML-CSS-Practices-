Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crisp.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  4. Drain spaghetti, add to the pancetta, and mix well.
  5. Pour egg mixture over spaghetti, stirring quickly until eggs thicken but do not scramble.
  6. Serve immediately with a generous amount of black pepper.
